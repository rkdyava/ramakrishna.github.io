Career at Medyantronics

We are a dynamic people of enthusiastic working environment and very open-minded for new and innovative ideas.


Medyantronics expecting from you

You will have a passion for developing software solutions to applied software mathematics problems. We would expect a at least a bachelor’s degree in computer science, engineering, mechatronics, robotics or equivalent. You’ll be enthusiastic, curious, energetic and proactive – and interested in what SS Innovations is seeking to achieve


Responsibilities:
Working within an exceptional team, you will be excepted to:

· Demonstrate a passion for using theoretical understanding to develop applied software
· Apply a strong mathematical background to real-world problems
· Develop and implement creative solutions based on different technologies and key requirements
· Demonstrate a rigorous attention to detail
· Apply best practice for software development
· You may expect to work on problems as varied as robot kinematics, sensor calibration, control, signal processing, telemetry data analysis and manufacturing test.

  • Mechanical Intern
  • Marketing Intern
